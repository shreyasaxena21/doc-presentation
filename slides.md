## ⚙️ Algorithmic Complexity

We analyze performance using **Big-O notation**:

### Examples

- Linear Search:  
  $$
  T(n) = O(n)
  $$

- Merge Sort:  
  $$
  T(n) = O(n \log n)
  $$

- Brute-force Matching:  
  $$
  T(n, m) = O(n \cdot m)
  $$
